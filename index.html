NEOVISION

Our product contains a sender device and a receiver device. The sender looks like contact lens and have a micro camera in it. The receiver can be any device that accepts signal inputs. When the user moves his/her eyes, the sender will capture his sight using the camera and the image would be updated on the receiver in real time. This is an usage of IOT technology.

Learn More

Remote communication

Despite of the communications between patients and his/her guardians, our device can make daily issues such as shopping easy to accomplish. Imagine a young couple, who agrees to buy their kid a fancy robot. However they failed to remember it until they got home. The kid cried for the toy and the parents may have to drive to the shop again to buy the toy. With our device, if a person wants to remind his/her family something, the person can simply focus his/her sight on an image of the thing. This aid from others can avoid a person from forgetting things, and is more efficient than communicating through mobile phones or emails.

Replace the role of electronic devices

As we stated in the first paragraphs, it is hard for a disabled person to use a mouse or a remote control. The receiver is a TV or a computer under this circumstance, and the sender functions as a mouse. The user can roll his/her eyes to scroll the screen and blinks as clicks. However, this function may be not so stable, as blinking is mostly unconscious and difficult to control.

Avoid direct contacts in public places

Nowadays, people are advised not to contact with others closely to avoid being infected by the coronavirus. However it may be easy to reduce human-human contacts, human-machine contacts are always unavoidable and this behaviour can also increase a person’s risk of inflection. Users can use our device to interact with machines instead. The gaze of a user to the receiver can trigger the wanted function. For example, a passenger with his/her device connected to a elevator, the person can select the destination floor by blinking to the receiver.

File recordings

As the function is based on a micro camera, the sender can also record first-person perspective videos. This can make files recorded even when the user’s hands are fully occupied. But this may lead to a concern of important file stolen in companies or private information leaking for individuals. These problems should be taken seriously because once the video is recorded, it risks the danger of being viewed by the unwanted person.

Family security ensurance

A monitor is usually fixed in one room of a house and have many blind spots. Using our devices as a monitor makes it possible to change the views dynamically. With the lens set onto a pet’s eyes, the pet can help to secure the house by sharing its sights.
